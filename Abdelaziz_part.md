##  Electrical System (Power Distribution)
This section covers the power architecture of the robotic system. A 12V power supply is used as the main source, distributing power directly to high-voltage components and through buck converters to safely step down voltage for lower-voltage devices such as the Raspberry Pi and platform motors. 

It also includes current consumption analysis of all components to ensure proper sizing of the power supply. A 12V source rated around 30A is selected to handle peak loads and maintain system reliability and safety.
[Electrical Architecture](electrical_Abdelaziz.pdf)

##  Mechanical Structure
This section describes the physical design of the robot, including the wheeled base, rotating platform, arm links, joints, and end-effector (gripper). The structure is designed to be both strong and lightweight, using materials like aluminium to reduce motor load while maintaining precision.

The design emphasizes stability, smooth multi-directional movement, proper load distribution, and ease of assembly and modification.
