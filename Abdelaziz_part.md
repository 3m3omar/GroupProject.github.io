##  Electrical System (Power Distribution)
This part discusses the power system of the robotic system. The main power supply is a 12 V supply, which supplies power directly to high-voltage equipment, and via buck converters, to safely reduce high voltage to lower-voltage equipment like the Raspberry Pi and platform motors. 

It also comprises of current consumption analysis of all the components to enable an adequate sizing of the power supply. To meet peak loads and ensure the system reliability and safety, a 12V source of approximately 30A is chosen.
[Electrical Architecture](electrical_Abdelaziz.pdf)

##  Mechanical Structure
This section will explain the physical layout of the robot, such as the wheel base, rotating platform, arm links, joints, and end-effector (gripper). The design will be that of a strong and lightweight structure by incorporation of materials such as aluminium to decrease the weight of the motor and still retain accuracy.

The design focuses on stability, easy multi-directional operation, adequate distribution of loads, and assembly and modification.
